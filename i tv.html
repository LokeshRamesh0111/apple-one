Questions? Answers.

How do I sign up for Apple One?

You can sign up for Apple One from your iPhone, iPad, Mac, or Apple TV in Settings under your Apple ID. Choose the plan that best fits your needs and start your subscription instantly.

What does it cost? Is it a monthly or annual subscription?

Apple One is billed monthly. The price varies depending on the plan you choose — Individual, Family, or Premier. You can cancel anytime.

How do I access my Apple One subscriptions?

All Apple One services are available in their respective apps, like Music, TV, iCloud, and more. Simply sign in with your Apple ID to access them.

Do I get a free trial? And what does it include?

Yes! New subscribers receive a free one-month trial for any services they don’t already have, allowing you to explore all the benefits of Apple One.

What if I need more iCloud storage?

If you need more storage, you can upgrade your iCloud+ plan anytime, even while using Apple One. Your total storage will include both plans combined.

Can I share with my family? How do I set it up?

Yes. Apple One Family and Premier plans support Family Sharing with up to five members. You can set it up in your iPhone or iPad Settings under Family Sharing.

How do I switch between individual subscriptions and Apple One subscriptions?

You can manage or switch your plan anytime from your device Settings under Subscriptions. Your billing will automatically adjust for the selected plan.

* The Apple One free trial includes only subscriptions that you are not currently using through a free trial or a subscription. Plan automatically renews after trial until cancelled. Restrictions and other terms apply.
